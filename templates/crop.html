Onion Cultivation Guide

Optimal Planting Seasons:
Region Best Planting Time Harvest Time
North India October-November April-May
South India May-June October-December
Central India November-December March-April
Pest Management:
Natural Thrips Control

Neem Oil Spray: Mix 5ml neem oil + 2ml liquid soap per liter of water. Spray every 10 days.

Garlic-Chili Spray: Blend 100g garlic + 100g chili in 1L water, strain and dilute 1:10 before spraying.

Chemical Fungal Disease Control

Dithane M-45: 2g per liter water for purple blotch. Spray at 15-day intervals.

Bavistin: 1g per liter water for stemphylium blight.

Pest Management:
Natural Fruit Borer Control

Neem Seed Kernel Extract: 5% solution sprayed weekly during fruiting.

Trichoderma viride: Soil application @ 2.5kg/ha mixed with FYM.

Chemical Early Blight Control

Mancozeb: 2g per liter at 10-day intervals from 3-4 week after planting.

Imidacloprid: 0.5ml per liter for whitefly control.

Rice Cultivation Guide

Optimal Planting Seasons:
Season Sowing Time Harvest Time Varieties
Kharif June-July November-December Swarna, MTU 1010
Rabi November-December March-April BPT 5204, WGL 14
Summer January-February April-May Intan, IR 64
Pest Management:
Natural Stem Borer Control

Neem Cake: Apply 250kg/ha in standing water.

NSKE 5%: Spray during evening hours at 15-day intervals.

Chemical Blast Disease Control

Tricyclazole: 1g per liter at disease appearance.

Carbendazim: 1g per liter for sheath blight.

Cotton Cultivation Guide

Optimal Planting Seasons:
Region Sowing Time Harvest Time
North Zone April-May October-December
Central Zone June-July December-February
South Zone May-June November-January
Pest Management:
Natural Bollworm Control

Nuclear Polyhedrosis Virus (NPV): 250 LE/ha mixed with 0.1% teepol.

Neem Oil 3%: Spray at 15-day intervals from square formation.

Chemical Sucking Pest Control

Acephate 75% SP: 1.5g per liter for aphids and whiteflies.

Spinosad 45% SC: 0.3ml per liter for spotted bollworm.
