Rice Cultivation Guide

Optimal Planting Seasons:
Season Sowing Time Harvest Time Varieties
Kharif June-July November-December Swarna, MTU 1010
Rabi November-December March-April BPT 5204, WGL 14
Summer January-February April-May Intan, IR 64
Soil Requirements:

Rice grows best in clayey loam soil with good water retention capacity. Ideal pH range: 5.5-6.5.

Pest Management:
Natural Stem Borer Control

Neem Cake: Apply 250kg/ha in standing water.

NSKE 5%: Spray during evening hours at 15-day intervals.

Chemical Blast Disease Control

Tricyclazole: 1g per liter at disease appearance.

Carbendazim: 1g per liter for sheath blight.

Cultivation Tips:
  • Maintain 2-3cm water depth during vegetative phase
  • Practice System of Rice Intensification (SRI) for higher yields
  • Apply FYM @ 5-10 tons/ha before planting
  • Use certified seeds for better germination

Maize Cultivation Guide

Optimal Planting Seasons:
Region Sowing Time Harvest Time
North India June-July September-October
South India October-November February-March
Central India June-July September-October
Soil Requirements:

Maize requires well-drained loamy soil with pH between 6.0-7.5. Avoid waterlogged conditions.

Pest Management:
Natural Stem Borer Control

Neem Oil 3%: Spray at 15-day intervals from 3rd week.

Trichogramma: Release @ 50,000/ha at 10 day intervals.

Chemical Fall Armyworm

Spinetoram 11.7% SC: 0.5ml per liter at early infestation.

Cultivation Tips:
  • Plant spacing: 60cm between rows, 20cm between plants
  • Apply 120:60:40 kg NPK/ha for optimal yield
  • Practice crop rotation with legumes
  • Harvest when moisture content is 20-25%

Jute Cultivation Guide

Optimal Planting Seasons:
Season Sowing Time Harvest Time
Main Season March-April July-August
Soil Requirements:

Jute grows best in alluvial sandy loam soils with pH 6.0-7.5.

Pest Management:
Natural Stem Weevil

Neem Seed Kernel Extract 5%: Spray at 15-day intervals.

Chemical Stem Rot

Carbendazim 50% WP: 1g per liter.

Cultivation Tips:
  • Broadcast seeds at 5-6 kg/ha rate
  • Apply 40:20:20 kg NPK/ha as basal dose
  • Harvest at early pod stage for best fiber quality
  • Ret in clean water for 15-20 days for fiber extraction

Cotton Cultivation Guide

Optimal Planting Seasons:
Region Sowing Time Harvest Time
North Zone April-May October-December
Central Zone June-July December-February
South Zone May-June November-January
Soil Requirements:

Cotton thrives in deep, well-drained black cotton soils with pH 6.0-8.0.

Pest Management:
Natural Bollworm Control

Nuclear Polyhedrosis Virus (NPV): 250 LE/ha mixed with 0.1% teepol.

Neem Oil 3%: Spray at 15-day intervals from square formation.

Chemical Sucking Pest Control

Acephate 75% SP: 1.5g per liter for aphids and whiteflies.

Spinosad 45% SC: 0.3ml per liter for spotted bollworm.

Cultivation Tips:
  • Plant at 60cm row spacing with 30cm plant spacing
  • Apply 80:40:40 kg NPK/ha in split doses
  • Practice topping (removal of terminal bud) to control height
  • Harvest in 2-3 pickings when bolls open

Coconut Cultivation Guide

Optimal Planting Seasons:
Region Planting Time Harvest Time
Coastal Areas May-June Year-round after 5-6 years
Soil Requirements:

Coconut thrives in sandy loam soils with pH 5.0-8.0.

Pest Management:
Natural Rhinoceros Beetle

Neem Cake: Apply 1kg per palm annually.

Biological Control: Use Metarhizium anisopliae fungus.

Chemical Eriophyid Mite

Dicofol 18.5% EC: 2.5ml per liter.

Cultivation Tips:
  • Plant at 7.5m x 7.5m spacing in pits filled with FYM
  • Apply 1kg NPK mixture per palm annually
  • Irrigate young palms at 4-5 day intervals
  • Harvest when nuts are 11-12 months old

Season Planting Time Harvest Time

Natural Papaya Ring Spot Virus

Muskmelon Cultivation Guide

Optimal Planting Seasons:
Season Sowing Time Harvest Time
Summer January-February April-May
Rainy June-July September-October
Soil Requirements:

Muskmelon prefers well-drained sandy loam soils with pH 6.0-6.8.

Pest Management:
Natural Powdery Mildew

Baking Soda Spray: 1 tsp baking soda + 1L water + few drops of soap.

Chemical Fruit Fly

Malathion 50% EC: 2ml per liter as bait spray.

Cultivation Tips:
  • Plant on raised beds for better drainage
  • Space plants 1m apart in rows 1.5m apart
  • Irrigate regularly but avoid wetting foliage
  • Harvest when fruit slips easily from vine

Mango Cultivation Guide

Optimal Planting Seasons:
Region Planting Time Harvest Time
All India July-August March-July (varies by variety)
Soil Requirements:

Mango grows in a wide range of soils but prefers deep, well-drained loamy soils with pH 5.5-7.5.

Pest Management:
Natural Fruit Fly Control

Neem Oil 3%: Spray at fruit development stage.

Bait Traps: Use 5 traps per acre.

Chemical Anthracnose

Carbendazim 50% WP: 1g per liter.

Cultivation Tips:
  • Plant in pits of 1m³ filled with FYM and topsoil
  • Prune to maintain 3-4 main branches
  • Irrigate at 10-15 day intervals in non-rainy season
  • Harvest when shoulders develop and color changes

Banana Cultivation Guide

Optimal Planting Seasons:
Region Planting Time Harvest Time
South India Year-round 12-15 months after planting
North India June-July 12-15 months after planting
Soil Requirements:

Banana requires deep, well-drained loamy soils rich in organic matter with pH 6.0-7.5.

Pest Management:
Natural Sigatoka Leaf Spot

Neem Oil 3%: Spray at 15-day intervals.

Chemical Panama Wilt

Carbendazim: Soil drench @ 1g per liter.

Cultivation Tips:
  • Plant suckers at 1.5m x 1.5m spacing
  • Remove excess suckers, maintain 3-4 per clump
  • Provide support to bearing plants
  • Harvest when fingers are plump but still green
